The ‘Product Lister for eBay’ easily empowers you with effective and efficient product optimization from the WooCommerce store to the eBay marketplace store.

== Description ==

Map and list your products from the WooCommerce platform with the respective supported categories on eBay. With the high functioning lister plugin, the tedious tasks of product and inventory updates, re-listing of the product catalog becomes a breeze from a centralized dashboard.

==Highlighted features of Product Lister for eBay:==

* One-click Product Upload.
* eBay Product Stock Update.
* Propagate changes to WooCommerce products back to eBay with our Product Update option.
* Relist your WooCommerce product on eBay.
* End your eBay Product from your WooCommerce Store.
* Live product search using SKU or Product Title.
* Maximum stock option for new eBay Sellers.
* Make your Listings stand out using our Description Templates.
* Map eBay Item Specifics to Product Attributes or Custom Fields.

Note- The free plugin is made available without any hidden charges.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does Product Lister for eBay work with all eBay sites? =

Yes, it does. Except for eBay Motors.

= What type of products can I upload to eBay using Product Lister for eBay? =

You can upload both Simple and Variable products type to eBay but please keep in mind that some eBay Categories places restriction on whether or not you can upload Variable products to such categories.

= Do you offer technical support for Product Lister for eBay? =

Since Product Lister for eBay is a free plugin our scope of support may vary. But if you are having trouble setting up the plugin or you are having any issues with the functioning of the plugin you can contact us from the floating button inside our plugin's page, in your WordPress site, or using the Support links given on this page. We will try our best to fix issues for you.

= Can I import my products from eBay to WooCommerce using Product Lister for eBay? =

No. Product Lister for eBay only supports uploading your products *from* WooCommerce *to* eBay. Automatic product import functionality is offered in our Full Version.

= How is the Product Lister for eBay different from other offerings in the Market? =

Our setup process is based on eBay Product Profiles which enables you to configure the Plugin once and then with One-Click you can upload your Products to eBay. This is vastly different from other 3rd Party plugins which involves product level configuration.

== Installation ==

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Product Lister for eBay , log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type *Product Lister for eBay* and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Product Lister for eBay plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking *Install Now*.

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our Product Lister for eBay plugin and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).
